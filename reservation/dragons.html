Night Fury


Fast, strong, stealthy and fiercely intelligent, a dragon made move with speed, and a fantastic guard too. They are also fantastic with kids, and great for a laugh, at least when their teeth aren't out


Deadly Nadder


This dragon shares quite a bit with your garden variety bird. Keep this one away from mirrors, unless you want it to preen itself for hours. The Nadder has tail spikes that it can launch with great precision, great for home improvment projects and stopping your enemies in their tracks!


Monstrous Nightmare


This beast has a very warm disposition. It has a habit of lighting itself on fire, but this makes a fantastic seat warmer in the more arctic regions you may want to explore!


Whispersing/Screaming Death


The Whispering and Screaming Death are burrowing dragons, so if you need underground transportation, then this is the dragon for you! Can you dig it?
